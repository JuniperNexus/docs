# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What is Juniper Nexus Bot?

Juniper Nexus Bot is a Discord bot designed to enhance functionality within Juniper Nexus servers. It provides various features such as moderation tools, utility commands, event management, and more.

### Can I use Juniper Nexus Bot on my own Discord server?

Juniper Nexus Bot is specifically tailored for Juniper Nexus servers and is not available for direct invitation. However, you can clone the bot's code from our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/JuniperNexus/discord-bot) and host it on your own server.

## Troubleshooting

### The bot is not responding to commands. What should I do?

Ensure that Juniper Nexus Bot is properly configured and has the necessary permissions to read messages and send replies in the channels where commands are used. Check the bot's status and connectivity to Discord servers.

### I encountered an error with a specific command. How do I report it?

If you encounter any issues or errors with Juniper Nexus Bot commands, please review the bot's GitHub repository or community documentation for troubleshooting steps. For further assistance, you can reach out to the developer community or open an issue on GitHub.

## Additional Support

### Where can I get more help or support?

For additional assistance, visit our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/JuniperNexus/discord-bot) for documentation, community discussions, and project updates related to Juniper Nexus Bot.
